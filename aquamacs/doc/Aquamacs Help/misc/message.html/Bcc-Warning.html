2.7.7 Bcc Warning

The Bcc header is meant to hide recipients of messages. However, when encrypted messages are used, the e-mail addresses of all Bcc-headers are given away to all recipients without warning, which is a bug, see https://debbugs.gnu.org/cgi/bugreport.cgi?bug=18718.
